# Kingdom Area Pack

Kingdom keeps the current town travel map and adds a forest-edge portal. Runtime data lives in `manifest.js`; this document and `area.yaml` are the authoring description.

## Gameplay

- Existing town scenes, shops, and help quests remain unchanged.
- `Luminara Castle` is a portal marker back to Castle.
- `Forest Path` is a portal marker to the Forest area.

## Asset Status

- Uses existing `assets/kingdom-map2.webp` and map actor assets.
- The forest-edge marker is data-driven and does not add new final map art.
